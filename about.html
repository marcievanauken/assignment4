Hello!

I am a graphic designer currently working in my chosen field. My alma mater is Northeastern University where I studied graphic design and had two 6-month co-operative experiences at both the Boston Globe doing editorial design, as well as at a small design firm called Metropolis Creative. I now have 4 years of professional experience since graduation and have an exceptional appreciation for all things design.

I have experience in all elements of print design but have been simultaneously finding a new place in my heart for web development. I am masterful in the Adobe Creative suite programs including InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator and Bridge. I have knowledge in HTML, CSS and JQuery and have excellent skills understanding all content management systems such as Drupal and Shopify.
